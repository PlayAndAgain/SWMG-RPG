Shadow Wizard Money Gang RPG :
Silly project with no pretention, just trying to make a simple RPG with Pyxel, based on the Shadow Wizard Money Gang Meme
CONTROLS (at the time):
Arrows to move 
Spacebar to Toggle the fight menu demo, spacebar again to escape it
